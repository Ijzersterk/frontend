DSVK IJzersterk

We: Daniël Colon, Ruud Kassing, Pouja Nikray and Daniël Schooneveld, started the association in 2015 with the following goals:

  1. To share reliable and trustworthy information about strength training in general.
  2. To share knowledge and expertise between athletes of different disciplines.
  3. To participate in competitions together.
  4. To give seminars and trainings.
  5. And most of all: to train together and to motivate each other in accomplishing our personal goal(s).

These points are what we offer to you when you join the association. Each Saturday we can train at the VKR (next to the entrance of the Sports Unit), there we can help you with technique and other issues.

The sports that are practised within the association are powerlifting, Olympic weightlifting and strongman.

Coaching

The current coaches are: Ruud Kassing, Joey Ma and Pouja Nikray. We have failed, tried and have huge amount of experience build up over the years. Besides lifting, we are also knowledgeable about nutrition, programming and competition preparation.

When you are a member of the association you can be trained by one of our coaches. If you are in need of instructions for a certain lift you can contact one the coaches. The coaching time slots are posted every week on Monday in the facebook group.

For help with programming, nutrition and or competition preparation, you can contact us by e-mail, whatsapp or poke us in he gym for help.

So make sure to use our knowledge!
